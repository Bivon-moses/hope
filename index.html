About us

Foundation of hope is a youth based organization which was established in October 2006 in KIBERA. It is a registered legal entity with the office of the district, Gender and social development officer. The certificate Registration No. is DGSD/NWD/5/4/09(38). This is an initiative of young professional change agents with a desire to use their skills and talents for the benefit of the greater society. Foundation of hope aims at empowering and transforming the communities to enhance their social and economical development and ensure a sustainable environmental integrity, justice and overall development.

Founder

David Dinda

David Dinda, the founder of the Foundation of Hope, is a visionary leader with a deep commitment to making a positive impact on society. Since establishing the foundation in 2006, he has demonstrated a passion for addressing pressing issues and creating meaningful change.

Under David's guidance, the Foundation of Hope has flourished into a dynamic and influential force for good, touching the lives of many and leaving a lasting legacy. His ability to inspire and mobilize a team of like-minded individuals, as well as engage with stakeholders, has been instrumental in the foundation's success.

Latest News

Foundation of Hope actively engages in community services, notably excelling in initiatives such as garbage collection and various impactful projects. the organization diligently contributes to waste management, ensuring cleaner and healthier surroundings. Their participation in garbage collection not only fosters a cleaner community but also reflects a dedication to sustainable practices. Beyond waste management, Foundation of Hope extends its outreach to diverse community projects, showcasing a holistic approach to service. Through these endeavors, the foundation exemplifies a profound commitment to uplifting the community, fostering a sense of shared responsibility, and making a tangible, positive impact on the lives of those they serve.

Foundation of Hope took center stage at the Youth for Christ Got Talent, clinching the top spot as the best traditional dance group. With rhythmic precision and cultural authenticity, earning the coveted first position. Their performance was a vibrant celebration of tradition and artistry, showcasing the richness of their cultural heritage. The victory not only honors their exceptional dance skills but also underscores their dedication to preserving and promoting traditional arts. Foundation of Hope stands as a beacon, not just as champions in a competition, but as proud custodians of their cultural legacy, inspiring others with the beauty of tradition.
